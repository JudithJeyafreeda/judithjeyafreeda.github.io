# Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach is grounded in:

- **Active Learning** — Students learn by doing, experimenting, and building.  
- **Research Integration** — I connect foundational concepts to real-world NLP and AI applications.  
- **Inclusive Pedagogy** — I adapt explanations and exercises to diverse learning backgrounds.  
- **Human-Centered Design** — Especially in web technologies, I emphasize accessibility and usability.  
- **Iterative Skill Development** — Students progressively build confidence through structured practice.
